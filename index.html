Ranchofest

Linden's biggest festival not based around produce

Groovers and groovettes!

The sacred hour is nearly upon us. We want to thank you for supporting us in our venture to curate radical undulations, and we can’t wait to show you what we’ve got in store. A night of staring at flashing lights and oscillating our ear drums awaits. In anticipation of dancing to like a bajillion songs, the good people of Ranchofest have prepared this pamphlet to help guide you through your spirit quest. As participants in this historic occasion, we ask that you all do your part to create safe and respectful environment. Please be considerate of other’s space and the land on which we have the privilege of dancing. Above all, we encourage all attendants to bring smiles and funky dance moves.

Ranchofest is a DONATION BASED gathering, so any and all help is appreciated. Recommended donation is $20, but feel free to donate more if you want! This ensures we can keep this going for years to come. If you would like to bring a +1, please let the person who invited you know.

What to expect

Camping:

There will be a designated tent camping area. Please be mindful of the space around you and considerate of others when setting up your tent. If you prefer to sleep in your car, you may do so in the parking lot.

Amenities:

  • Multiple water stations
  • Clean porta potties
  • Grill for cooking dinner?
  • Chill out area
  • This event is pack it in, pack it out.

Food & Drink:

BYO food, snacks, and libations! There is a grill available and we will provide some charcoal, but you should consider bringing your own grill supplies if you are set on grilling. Definitely bring an ice chest to keep your things coooool.

Landscape:

The lawn on the ranch is slightly uneven. Be mindful and cautious of where you step on the property - we don’t want any twisted ankles!

Parking

We will have a designated parking area for cars, approximately 300 feet away from the general festival grounds and tent camping area. Be prepared to carry your camping stuff from the parking lot to the camping area.

For environmental purposes (and fun!), we encourage you to carpool with friends coming from the same area.
